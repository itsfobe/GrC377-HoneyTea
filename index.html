Hello, Welcome to Honey Tea!

The Authentic Fruit Tea House

At Honey Tea, we strive to bring you quality drinks made out of quality ingredients. We only use purees and juices made from fresh fruit, and real cane sugar to flavor and sweeten our drinks -- NO artificial sweeteners! Our motto, “Healthy Drinks, Happy Life,” is centered around the mission of bringing authentic drinks to the table. We have a large selection to cater towards your fruit preferences, as well as milk teas, smoothies, slushies, and more!
